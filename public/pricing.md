## What is Wholesale Beverage Distribution?

Wholesale beverage distribution is the business-to-business supply of packaged drinking water, soft drinks, juices, and energy drinks in bulk quantities. A wholesale distributor sources products directly from manufacturers and supplies them to offices, retailers, hotels, event organizers, and institutions at volume-based discounted prices.

## Pricing Factors

Wholesale prices depend on:
1. **Order volume** (higher volume = deeper discount)
2. **Product brand** (premium brands vs. economy brands)
3. **Package size** (larger formats = better per-liter value)
4. **Payment terms** (advance payment may unlock additional discounts)
5. **Delivery frequency** (scheduled recurring orders get preferential rates)
